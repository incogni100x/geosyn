About Us

Who We Are

Geosyn RAP Consultants is a premier mining and resource consulting firm dedicated to helping clients discover, develop, and maximize the value of their mineral projects. With over 150 years of combined industry experience, our team of geologists, landmen, engineers, and consultants brings unmatched expertise to every project.

We have successfully staked over 25,800 mineral claims and supported more than 3,500 projects worldwide, building a proven track record of reliability, accuracy, and results. Our experience spans a wide range of projects, from early-stage exploration to large-scale aggregate and construction initiatives.

Meet Our Team
