Mineral Exploration

Mineral Claim Staking

We specialize in accurate, compliant, and efficient claim staking. Our Landman services ensure every claim is properly recorded, maintained, and strategically managed—helping clients secure valuable mineral rights with confidence.

Claim Guard

Our Claim Guard service protects your mineral assets from lapses, oversights, or third-party interference. We handle renewals, filings, and compliance monitoring so your claims remain secure and legally sound at all times.

Technical Reports

We prepare high-quality technical reports that meet both regulatory requirements and investor expectations. From NI 43-101 style documentation to feasibility studies, our reports provide clear, reliable, and professional insights that support project financing and decision-making.

GIS Services

Using advanced Geographic Information Systems (GIS), we deliver precise mapping, spatial analysis, and data visualization for mineral exploration and project planning. Our GIS solutions give clients a clear understanding of terrain, geology, and claim distribution to guide smarter decisions.

Geological Services

Our team of seasoned geologists offers comprehensive exploration and evaluation services, including field mapping, sampling, and resource estimation. We combine hands-on expertise with modern technology to accurately assess and unlock the value of your resource projects.
