Services

Tailored Solutions for Mining & Resource Projects

At Geosyn RAP Consultants, we provide a full suite of services designed to add value at every stage of your project.

Mine Planning & Consulting

We develop tailored mine planning strategies that maximize resource recovery while minimizing costs and environmental impact. Our experts integrate modern software and decades of field experience to deliver:

  • • Open-pit and underground mine design
  • • Production scheduling and optimization
  • • Feasibility studies and project evaluations
  • • Cost modeling and risk assessments

Claim Staking & Landman Services

Securing mineral rights is the foundation of exploration success. We provide complete landman and claim staking services to protect your assets and streamline development:

  • • Mineral claim staking across multiple jurisdictions
  • • Land acquisition and title verification
  • • Claim renewals and compliance monitoring
  • • Boundary surveys and documentation

Construction Aggregate Consulting

Our team supports construction, infrastructure, and development firms by providing reliable aggregate solutions. We help identify, evaluate, and optimize aggregate resources to keep projects moving:

  • • Aggregate sourcing and quality testing
  • • Market analysis and demand forecasting
  • • Supply chain and logistics support
  • • Compliance and permitting guidance

Mineral Market Analysis

We deliver actionable intelligence to help clients make informed investment and development decisions in volatile resource markets:

  • • Commodity price forecasting
  • • Regional and global market assessments
  • • Competitor and demand analysis
  • • Investment and acquisition advisory

Geological & GIS Services

Our geological expertise combined with advanced GIS tools ensures accurate data-driven decisions throughout the project lifecycle:

  • • Geological mapping and structural interpretation
  • • Core logging and sampling programs
  • • Remote sensing and terrain modeling
  • • Resource estimation and exploration targeting

From claim staking to market analysis, we help clients maximize the value of their mineral resource projects worldwide.
