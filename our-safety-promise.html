Our Safety Promise

Safety First, Always

At Geosyn RAP Consultants, safety is more than a policy—it’s a culture. We believe that every employee, contractor, and stakeholder has the right to a safe working environment, and we are committed to achieving zero harm across all our operations.

Our Safety Principles

  • People First – Protecting lives is our highest priority.
  • Preventive Mindset – We identify risks before they become incidents.
  • Shared Responsibility – Safety is everyone’s responsibility, from leadership to field staff.
  • Continuous Improvement – We constantly review, adapt, and strengthen our safety practices.

Key Safety Measures

Comprehensive Training: Every team member undergoes rigorous onboarding and ongoing safety training, tailored to their role.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE): We enforce the mandatory use of high-standard PPE in all operational areas.

24/7 Monitoring & Reporting: On-site supervisors and digital tools help track compliance and flag potential hazards in real time.

Emergency Response Plans: Preparedness drills and response teams ensure we are ready for any situation.

Health & Wellness Programs: From medical screenings to mental health support, we prioritize the overall well-being of our workforce.

Our Commitment in Action

  • •Regular field audits and toolbox talks keep safety front of mind.
  • •Digital reporting dashboards provide visibility for leadership and teams.
  • •Partnering with clients to align site-specific protocols with our standards.

Certifications & Standards

We align with leading global frameworks and standards, including:

  • ISO 45001: Occupational Health & Safety Management
  • International Council on Mining & Metals (ICMM): Safety Charter
  • Mine Health & Safety Act: Compliance for applicable regions
  • OSHA Standards: Adherence across U.S. operations

Safety Performance

Over 2 million man-hours without Lost Time Injury (LTI) across operations in the last 3 years.

100% workforce participation in quarterly safety drills.

15% year-on-year reduction in reported incidents through proactive monitoring and response.

From claim staking to market analysis, we help clients maximize the value of their mineral resource projects worldwide.
